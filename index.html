Interesting Facts About Camels

  • Camels can have one or two humps
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand from getting into their eyes
  • Camels can go up to seven months in the desert without drinking water

Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their heads
  • Chameleons mostly live in the rain forests and deserts of Africa
  • Chameleons have the fastest tongue speed

Interesting Facts About Pigeons

  • Pigeons are the smartest of all bird species
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Bear

Interesting Facts About Bears

  • Bears are carnivoran mammals of the family Ursidae
  • Bears grieve deeply for others
  • Grizzly bears possess a biting force of over 1200 PSI, which is enough to crush a bowling ball or an iron skillet

Panda

Interesting Facts About Pandas

  • Giant pandas spend 10-16 hours a day feeding, mainly on bamboo
  • Pandas poo 40 times a day
  • Newborn pandas are pink

Cicada

Interesting Facts About Cicadas

  • Cicadas live for 13-17 years, the longest lifespan of any insect
  • Cicadas can't eat
  • Cicadas’ wings are water and bacteria proof
